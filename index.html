About Me:

Skills:

Programming:

Following my training at Epicodus, I will be able to skillfully program in PHP, JavaScript, and Drupal, navigate a BASH console, and manage projects in GitHub. I also have experience setting up and working inside of WordPress themes.

Other Skills:

  • Task management.
  • Communication.
  • Teamwork.

Job Experience:

After graduating high school, I was trained as a test processor and tutor for a local Christian academy. One year later, I was trained and hired as a content developer for an Inbound marketing agency in Washington.

During my time as a writer, I had the opportunity to create several websites for clients using WordPress. Inspired by the beauty, complexity, and power of many impressive websites, I realized the kind of potential these websites could have for small businesses and non-profits. Driven by a desire to create functional, impressive, and memorable websites, I chose to pursue a career in website design and development.

Background:

I am one of five girls, raised by my loving parents in Oregon City, OR. Outside of programming, I enjoy music, sports, computer games, art, and writing.

After receiving my high school diploma, I took a year of college classes at Clackamas Community College before deciding to attend Epicodus.
